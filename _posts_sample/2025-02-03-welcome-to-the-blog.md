This is an example post with a **featured image**. Set `image` (or `featured_image`) in the front matter to show a hero image above the content.

You can use **Markdown** (Kramdown) for content. The theme uses readable typography and a constrained line length for comfortable reading.

## Configuration

Edit `_config.yml` to set:

- **Site**: name, title, description, logo, favicon, baseurl
- **Integrations**: Disqus (`disqus_shortname`), Google Analytics (`google_analytics`), ads (`ads`, `ads_client`, `ads_slot`)
- **Pagination**: `paginate`, `paginate_path`
- **Permalink**: `permalink`
- **Authors**: add entries in `_data/authors.yml` and reference with `author: id` in posts

Add your featured image at `assets/images/posts/welcome.jpg`, or use a **full URL** (e.g. from [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com)) in `image` in the post front matter.
